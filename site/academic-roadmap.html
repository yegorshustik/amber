Overview

Amber Council's Academic Roadmap is a single, accountable program that takes a student from where they are today to the strongest institution they can realistically reach. It is not a one-off application service — it is ongoing guidance, built around your child and managed end to end.

Everything below is placeholder content to demonstrate the page system. Final copy will be supplied and edited in place.

How it works

The roadmap moves through clear stages. You always know where your child stands and what comes next.

Assessment

We start with an honest, ability-weighted assessment: academic profile, interests, and realistic targets. No overselling — we map what is genuinely reachable.

Placement & preparation

We shortlist the right schools or universities, manage the full application and deadlines, and prepare the student for exams and interviews.

Post-enrollment support

After enrollment we stay on — communicating with the institution, monitoring progress, and handling issues as they arise. This is the signature layer of the program.

What's included

  • 24/7 access to your advisor throughout the journey
  • Unlimited calls and meetings — online or offline
  • Full accountability for every stage of the process
  • Work with any institution, not only partner schools
  • Experience with top-tier universities

Is this for you?

Who we work with.

The Academic Roadmap is designed for families who want a managed, highly structured path to top-tier international education. It is ideal for students aged 11–17 aiming for selective boarding schools in the UK, Switzerland, or the US, and those targeting Ivy League, Oxbridge, or Russell Group universities.
