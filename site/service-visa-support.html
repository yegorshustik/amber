Overview

Relocating a child abroad to study involves complex visa regulations, detailed financial requirements, and strict deadlines. Mistakes can delay or jeopardize enrollment. Our Visa Support service takes the stress out of the process, ensuring all documentation is filed correctly and on time.

Navigating the rules

Immigration policies for student and tourist visas change constantly. What worked last year may not apply today. We stay up-to-date with embassy requirements for key destinations (UK, EU, USA, Canada) to ensure compliance.

Our approach

We do not just fill out forms. We evaluate your family's situation, determine the most suitable visa category, analyze financial requirements, and build a clear timeline aligned with the school or university start date.

Key visa areas

We support families with two primary visa types:

  • Student visas: Full guidance for the student, including school-sponsored certificates (like CAS in the UK or I-20 in the US) and biometric booking.
  • Parent / Visitor visas: Visas for parents to accompany their child at the start of the school year or visit during term time.
