Privacy Policy

We take confidentiality seriously. This policy explains how we collect, protect, and use your personal information during your collaboration with Amber Council.

Information we collect

We only collect personal information that is necessary to map and walk your child's academic path. This includes:

  • Contact details: Name, email address, telephone number, and residential country.
  • Student profile details: Age, current school grade, academic transcripts, language proficiencies, and extracurricular records.
  • Consultation notes: Information provided during calls, emails, or portfolio reviews.

How we use your data

Your data is processed exclusively to deliver our consulting services. We use it to:

  • Evaluate your child's profile and create the Academic Roadmap.
  • Coordinate applications to selected boarding schools and universities.
  • Communicate with you regarding application milestones and admissions tasks.

Confidentiality & security

Amber Council enforces a strict standard of discretion as a discipline. We protect your documents through secure, encrypted storage systems. We do not sell, rent, or distribute your personal information to third parties or marketing brokers.

Your rights

You have full ownership of your data. You may contact us at any time to request access to your records, ask for corrections, or request complete deletion of all personal data from our systems.

Policy updates

We may update this policy periodically to reflect changes in our operational procedures or privacy regulations. Any updates will be published directly on this page.
