Overview

Choosing the right boarding school is one of the most critical decisions in your child's academic journey. We find schools that match your child's academic profile, personality, and interests, and manage the entire placement process end-to-end to ensure they secure a place where they will thrive.

Why boarding schools matter

A good boarding school provides more than just rigorous academics; it offers a community, extracurricular breadth, and preparation for independent life. It is the strongest springboard for entry into elite universities worldwide.

Our approach

We do not believe in a one-size-fits-all list. We look at the student honestly — assessing their strengths, weaknesses, and potential. We recommend schools where they will be challenged but not overwhelmed, ensuring a productive and happy school experience.

The placement process

We break the placement down into clear, manageable steps:

  • Shortlisting: We select 3–5 candidate schools based on a detailed profile analysis.
  • Applications: We coordinate all document submissions, essay writing, and registration deadlines.
  • Preparation: We provide practice materials and coaching for entrance exams (like UKiset, ISEB, or SSAT) and school interviews.
  • Enrollment: We guide you through accepting the offer, deposits, and boarding arrangements.
