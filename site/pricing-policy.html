How our pricing works

We offer three tiers — Basic, Standard and Premium — covering the full journey, grouped into five stages. Each service is included at one of three levels:

  • Full — we handle it for you, end to end.
  • Info — we guide you and you complete it yourself.
  • — — not included in this tier.
