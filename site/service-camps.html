Overview

Summer and short-term programs abroad are more than just holidays — they are the first step toward international education. A well-chosen camp gives your child a taste of independence, builds language confidence in a native setting, and adds valuable credentials to their future school or university profile.

Why short-term programs matter

Top boarding schools and universities look for candidates with global experience. A camp at a top school or university is the easiest way to test whether your child is ready for boarding school life, and it builds their confidence dramatically.

Our approach

We do not work with mass-market tourist camps. Instead, we select academic, sports, or creative camps hosted by premier boarding schools and universities. We match the program to your child's personality, language level, and long-term academic path.

Selecting the right camp

Depending on your child's age and goals, we recommend one of three paths:

  • Language & Activity: For younger students (ages 9–13) to get comfortable with English and living away from home.
  • Academic Tasters: For older students to experience subjects like business, robotics, or literature in a boarding school environment.
  • University Preparation: Intensive courses at top universities (like Oxford, Cambridge, or Yale) to prepare for future applications.

Logistics & Safety

Sending a child abroad can be stressful. We take full responsibility for the logistics:

  1. Selecting vetted, secure programs with high staff-to-student ratios.
  2. Handling enrollment documents, visas, and health insurance.
  3. Arranging airport transfers and flights, with unaccompanied minor service if required.
  4. Remaining contactable 24/7 during the camp.
