Overview

Securing an offer is only the beginning of a student's journey. Adapting to a new country, a different educational system, and living independently can be overwhelming. Our Post-Enrollment Support is our signature service — we stay with your child after they enroll to ensure their long-term success and peace of mind.

Why ongoing support matters

Many students encounter difficulties in their first year: academic pressure, cultural adaptation, roommate conflicts, or navigation of school administration. Without proper guidance, small issues can spiral into major setbacks. We provide immediate, professional assistance when it's needed most.

Our approach

We do not wait for problems to arise. We maintain regular contact with the student, monitor their academic progress, and check in on their emotional well-being. We act as an active bridge between the school, the student, and the parents back home.

Support areas

Our post-enrollment assistance focuses on three core areas:

  • Academic monitoring: Reviewing school grades, coordinating with subject teachers, and organizing additional tutoring support when required.
  • Social & life guidance: Helping students settle into boarding life, resolve peer or dorm conflicts, and manage independent routines.
  • Parent liaison: Providing parents with structured updates on academic progress and school life, translating administrative requirements.

What's included

Our signature support service includes:

  1. 24/7 direct messaging and call access to your advisor for both student and parents.
  2. Scheduled monthly reviews of academic performance and well-being.
  3. Direct mediation with school houseparents, teachers, or administrators.
  4. Next-stage academic planning (GCSE/A-Level choices, early university guidance).
