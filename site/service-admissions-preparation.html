Overview

Selective boarding schools and top-tier universities have single-digit acceptance rates. Academic talent alone is rarely enough to stand out. Our Admissions Preparation service focuses on building a coherent application profile, sharpening exam performance, and mastering the interview.

Why preparation is critical

Entrance tests and interviews are designed to filter out memorization. Admissions committees look for critical thinking, intellectual curiosity, and cultural fit. Early, targeted preparation is the only way to build these skills naturally.

Our approach

We begin with diagnostic testing to map current ability against target school benchmarks. Then we design a personalized tutoring and coaching plan, focusing only on the gaps that matter.

Preparation areas

We prepare students across three key areas:

  • Entrance exams: Customized tutoring for standard tests (SAT, ACT, IELTS, CAT4, UKiset) and school-specific entrance papers.
  • Interview coaching: Interactive mock interviews mimicking the exact style of top schools, with videorecorded analysis.
  • Application essays: Guiding the student to find their unique voice and write compelling personal statements.

FAQ

Common questions.

When should we start preparing?

Ideally 12 to 18 months before the target enrollment date. This leaves enough time for meaningful improvement without burnout.

Do you guarantee admission if we take this service?

We guarantee that your child will be as competitive as realistically possible. True selective schools never offer guaranteed admission.

Are tutors native speakers?

Yes, we match students with specialized tutors, often alumni of the target educational systems or native speakers with extensive admissions experience.
