Choosing the right approach

Modular services vs. Academic Roadmap

While our modular services resolve immediate needs, the Academic Roadmap integrates all of them into a continuous, cohesive strategy. Use the table below to compare approaches.

Feature / Benefit Modular Services Academic Roadmap
Scope of work Single objective (e.g. camps, visa) End-to-end (from first school to university enrollment)
Accountability Project-based Continuous (we own the outcome of the path)
Advisor Availability During active project phases 24/7, year-round access to personal advisor
University Entrance Strategy Upon request Included as standard part of the roadmap
Pricing Model Fixed fee per module By annual consultation

Yes. Every service is available standalone, or combined into your Academic Roadmap.
